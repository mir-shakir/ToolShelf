Frequently Asked Questions

Is ToolShelf really free?

Yes! ToolShelf is completely free to use. No registration required, no hidden fees, no premium tiers. All features are available to everyone.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. All text processing happens locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device and is never sent to any servers.

Can I use it offline?

Yes! Once the page loads, ToolShelf works completely offline. Perfect for when you need to process text without internet access.

Are there any text size limits?

ToolShelf can handle large amounts of text efficiently. The only limit is your device's memory capacity.

Can I save my transformed text?

Yes! You can copy the results to your clipboard or download them as a text file for future use.

What browsers are supported?

ToolShelf works in all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. No plugins required.

How to Use ToolShelf Text Transformer

Quick Start:

  1. Type or paste your text in the Input Text area
  2. Select transformation options from the left sidebar
  3. See instant results in the Transformed Text area
  4. Copy or download your results when ready

Tips:

  • Use section reset buttons to clear specific transformation groups
  • Case transformations are mutually exclusive (only one at a time)
  • You can combine multiple operations and line transformations
  • Statistics show the difference between input and output
  • All processing happens locally - your data stays private

Keyboard Shortcuts:

  • Ctrl+L - Clear input
  • Ctrl+Enter - Copy output
  • Ctrl+D - Download output
  • Ctrl+R - Reset all transformations
