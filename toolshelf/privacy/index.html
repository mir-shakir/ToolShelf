Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 07, 2025

ToolShelf ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our website (https://toolshelf.tech). By using ToolShelf, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

1. What Information We Collect

a) Personal Data

We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as your name, email, or address) unless you voluntarily provide it via feedback forms or direct contact.

b) Usage Data

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized usage data such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Page visits and time spent
  • General location (e.g., country-level)
  • Interaction events (e.g., tool usage)

This data helps us understand how users interact with our tools and improve the experience.

c) Cookies

We use essential cookies to:

  • Remember user settings or actions
  • Track anonymized usage metrics via Google Analytics

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but some parts of the site may not function as intended.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data to:

  • Improve our tools and website performance
  • Monitor usage patterns
  • Ensure site security and stability

We do not sell or share your data with advertisers, third parties, or marketing platforms.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely using industry-standard practices. We do not retain any personal information on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

ToolShelf uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — anonymized usage tracking
  • Google Tag Manager — lightweight analytics integration

These services may set cookies or collect limited information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

  • You may request that we delete any personally submitted feedback or contact messages.
  • You can opt out of analytics tracking using browser extensions like Google Analytics Opt-out.

6. Children's Privacy

ToolShelf is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at https://toolshelf.tech/privacy. Significant changes will be communicated via a banner or notification.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your data or this policy:
